Help Bring Clean Water to Mtiéki, Bandjoun — and Launch a Citizen Movement

Because access to clean water should never depend on where you're born.

Our first goal: a deep water borehole for the people of Mtiéki, Cameroon.

This is a grassroots initiative led by the diaspora, the local community, and all who believe in a fairer future.

No grand political promises. No reliance on NGOs. Just citizens taking action — together, to fund real development projects with transparency and long-term vision (that of a continent building its future through democratic and collective efforts).

This isn’t just a one-time act. It’s the beginning of a long-term transformation.

Why this project matters

In Mtiéki, like in so many villages, clean water is a luxury. Children drink from rivers. Families walk kilometers daily. But together, we can change that. This isn’t just a well — it’s a matter of dignity.

What makes this initiative different

  • Every euro is traceable
  • All spending is documented (photos, receipts, updates)
  • The community decides future projects
  • Full transparency — to build trust, a repeatable model, and proof that another way is possible

What we’ll fund together

  • Deep water borehole + engineering
  • A durable pumping system
  • Public updates (photos, videos, reports)
  • A transparent, participative citizen platform

Why donate now?

If just 2,500 people give 2 € (≈ $2,15 / £1,70 / 1300 fcfa), we reach our goal.

2,500 people x 2 € = 1 borehole.
That’s it. And it’s huge.

We don’t care if you’re wealthy or modest, from the country or abroad — this project is for anyone who believes no child should go without drinking water in 2025.

How you can help:

  • Make a donation (even symbolic)
  • Share this message
  • Talk about it with others

Thank you for believing we can make change happen. Thank you for acting.
The future starts here — with one simple gesture and a powerful impact.
